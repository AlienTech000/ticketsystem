# How to Use the Ticket Management System

Welcome to the Ticket System! This application allows you to create, manage, and track tickets within your organization. Below are the instructions on how to use the application effectively.

## Features

1. **Create Ticket**:
   - Click the "Create Ticket" button in the sidebar.
   - Fill in the required fields: First Name, Last Name, Issue, and Priority.
   - Click "Submit" to create the ticket.

2. **View All Tickets**:
   - Click the "View All Tickets" button to display a list of all tickets in the system.

3. **Search Tickets**:
   - Click the "Search Tickets" button to look for tickets by first and last name.

4. **Close Ticket**:
   - Click the "Close Ticket" button to close an open ticket.
   - Enter the ticket ID of the ticket you wish to close.

5. **Reopen Ticket**:
   - Click the "Reopen Ticket" button to reopen a closed ticket.
   - Enter the ticket ID of the ticket you wish to reopen.

6. **Edit Ticket**:
   - Click the "Edit Ticket" button to modify an existing ticket.
   - Enter the ticket ID of the ticket you wish to edit and make the necessary changes.

7. **Delete Ticket**:
   - Click the "Delete Ticket" button to remove a ticket from the system.
   - Enter the ticket ID of the ticket you wish to delete and confirm the deletion.

8. **Exit**:
   - Click the "Exit" button to close the application.

## Tips

- Ensure that all required fields are filled when creating or editing a ticket.
- Keep the ticket IDs noted for easy management of your tickets.
- Use the search feature to quickly find tickets associated with specific individuals.

Thank you for using the Ticket System!
